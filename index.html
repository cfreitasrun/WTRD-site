About WTRD:

The West Texas Roller Derby league began in 2007, known then as the West Texas Roller Dollz. WTRD officially changed their name in 2017.

Practice with WTRD!

WTRD practices are held at The Fiesta Event Center at 1902 E 4th St, Lubbock, TX 79403. Practices are every Tuesday and Thursday from 6:30pm till 9:00pm. New skater (freshmeat) practices are Thursdays from 6:30pm till 7:00pm.
